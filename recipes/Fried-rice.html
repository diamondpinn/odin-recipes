Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Nigerian fried rice is a colourful and flavourful dish commonly served at celebrations and special occasions. It is made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, spices, and meat or seafood, giving it a rich taste and pleasant aroma.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Carrots
  • Green peas
  • Sweet corn
  • Green beans
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Chicken or shrimp
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cubes

Steps

  1. Wash and cook the rice until tender, then set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pot or frying pan.
  3. Add chopped onions and stir-fry until fragrant.
  4. Add diced carrots, green beans, peas, and sweet corn, and stir.
  5. Add curry powder, thyme, salt, and seasoning cubes.
  6. Add cooked chicken or shrimp and mix well.
  7. Add the cooked rice and stir-fry until evenly mixed.
  8. Adjust seasoning and cook for a few more minutes.
