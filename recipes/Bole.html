Bole Recipe

Description

Bole is a tasty street food, made up of locally found ingredients. Its roasted and hot spicy condiments makes it a must have for anyone seeking the pleasure of a local african delicacy.

Ingredients

  • Palm oil
  • Uziza leaf
  • Salt
  • Onions
  • Fish
  • Plantain
  • Yam
  • Garden eggs
  • Pepper
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Slice the ripe plantain into 4 parts.
  2. Peel and slice your yam and potatoes into small cube shapes.
  3. Wash the yam and poatoes.
  4. Place the yam, fish, potatoes and plantain on top the grill to roast.
  5. Place another small pot on fire.
  6. Add the pepper, onions, diced garden egg, salt, and uziza leaf to make stew.
  7. Take out the roasted items, put on a place and add a generous amout of stew.
